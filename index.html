About me

As an experienced Architect-Designer and a Design Lecture I bring a unique set of abilities and skills which may prove valuable in front-end design and development projects. I have experience in creative ways of thinking and problem solving - a clear thinker with an ability to see the "wood" from the "trees".
I create and design environments - spaces where users can interact, experience and understand. I translate client's needs into organization principals within a project's context, by understanding Form-Function Relations, Scale-Relations and System-Overview (designing a door knob while keeping a perspective of the whole building). Keeping true to the notion of Roman architecture critic Vitruvius, that good design should exhibit: "Firmness, Commodity, and Delight".

I Plan as an Architect, Think as a Designer, and Implement as a Developer.
I have a strong interest in Data-Visualization (d3.js) and 3D web applications (Three.js), continually evaluating and upgrading my skills in these fields.
I'm fascinated by technology but love nature, free-diving and kitesurfing.
I teach Design but believe that creativity can't be taught.
I believe you shouldn't always believe everything you think, but I also believe that the most important single thing, beyond discipline and creativity is daring to dare.
