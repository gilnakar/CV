About me

As an Architect-Designer I've gained a wide perception of human, environment, and technology factors and the role they play in understanding needs, developing design strategies and implementing solutions.

Teaching Design for more than 15 years has kept me young and taught me empathy, asking questions, creative conceptual broad-thinking, visual and verbal communication/presentation skills and sharp critique ability.

I sought more concrete knowledge and completed my MSc. Thesis in the field of Digital-Fabrication, understanding more about analytic research, realization processes, and painfully, about the ways of the academic world

I'm a member of COMAS School of Design Teaching Committee. I take part in developing the school's vision and involved in problem-solving and decision-making processes. Recently I've initiated and participated in developing a new academic program proposal in Information-Design.

I've completed a comprehensive Front-End-Development course at Netcraft Academy, discovered the "magic" of code and the endless potential of creative coding. I'm particularly fascinated by Data-Visualization (d3.js) and 3D web applications (Three.js), continually evaluating and upgrading my skills in these areas.

I understand that design and technology skills are only details in a Vision,
a vision of how we as humans should be,
followed by passion and determination to resolve it
effectively, efficiently and creatively.
